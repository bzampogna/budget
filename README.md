# budget
Budget PWA

## Draft Day — fantasy football draft assistant (`draft/`)

A second, separately installable PWA that lives at `draft/index.html`
(e.g. `https://<user>.github.io/budget/draft/`). Open it on your phone and
use **Add to Home Screen** to install it alongside the budget app.

What it does during a live snake draft:

- Configure your league once: teams, your pick slot, PPR / half-PPR /
  standard scoring, rounds, and starting roster (QB/RB/WR/TE/FLEX/K/DST).
- Tap **Draft** on each player as they're picked in your real draft; the app
  tracks the snake order, shows who's on the clock, and counts down to your
  pick.
- **Suggested Picks** ranks the best available players for *your* roster:
  starter/FLEX needs, players falling past their ADP, and positional tier
  scarcity ("last in RB Tier 3"), with K/DST held back until the late rounds.
- My Team view (auto slotted starters + bench), full draft board, search and
  position filters, undo, and everything saved in localStorage — close the
  app mid-draft and pick up where you left off. Works offline once installed.

Player rankings come from three sources, in order of preference:

1. **Live ADP** from the free fantasyfootballcalculator.com API, matched to
   your scoring format and league size (fetched on Start Draft, or anytime
   via Settings → Fetch live ADP).
2. **CSV import** — paste any rankings export (e.g. FantasyPros) in Settings.
3. **Built-in snapshot** — an offline fallback ranking baked into the app
   (consensus as of the end of the 2025 season; refresh with live ADP or a
   CSV for draft day).

Icons are generated by `scripts/make-draft-icons.py` (no dependencies).

## Bill reminder push notifications

A GitHub Action (`.github/workflows/bill-reminders.yml`) runs every morning,
reads the budget data from JSONBin, and sends a Web Push notification to every
subscribed device listing unpaid bills that are overdue or due within the
chosen reminder window.

### One-time setup

1. In the repo go to **Settings → Secrets and variables → Actions → New
   repository secret** and add these three secrets:
   - `JSONBIN_KEY` — your JSONBin master key (same one entered in the app's
     Cloud Sync screen)
   - `JSONBIN_BIN_ID` — your bin ID (shown in the app's Cloud Sync screen)
   - `VAPID_PRIVATE_KEY` — the private half of the push key pair (the public
     half is hardcoded in `budget.html` and `scripts/send-reminders.mjs`)
2. On each phone: open the app from the Home Screen → ⚙ Settings →
   **🔔 Bill Reminders** → Turn On Notifications. (iOS 16.4+ required, and the
   app must be installed via Add to Home Screen.)
3. Optional: test it from the **Actions** tab → "Bill reminders" →
   **Run workflow**. Nothing is sent unless a bill is actually due within the
   window.

The reminder window (1–7 days before the due date) is set in the app under
Bill Reminders and is shared by all devices. The send time is set by the cron
line in the workflow (UTC).

### Regenerating push keys

Generate a new P-256 VAPID pair, then update all three places together:
`VAPID_PUBLIC_KEY` in `budget.html`, the same constant in
`scripts/send-reminders.mjs`, and the `VAPID_PRIVATE_KEY` repo secret.
Devices must then re-enable notifications.
